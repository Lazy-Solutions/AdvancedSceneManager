## FAQ

- What is Advanced Scene Manager?

  - Advanced Scene Manager (ASM) is a scene manager for unity, that simplifies additive scene management.

- What _can_ ASM do?

  - Organize scenes into collections, which allows us to mass open / close scenes.
  - Open scene and collections from UnityEvent.
  - Easily set scenes or collections as persistent.
  - Preload scenes.
  - Loading screens.
  - More customizable splash screens.

- What _cannot_ ASM do?
  - Stream scenes - We're not a world streamer. ASM focuses on general scene management.
  - Preload multiple scenes at once - this is a unity limitation, not something we can work around.
